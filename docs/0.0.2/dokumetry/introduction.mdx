All LLM observability data collected by DokuMetry is sent directly to the Doku Platform for streamlined tracking.

## Features

- **Effortless Setup:** Integrate full-featured LLM usage monitoring into your application with just two lines of code.
- **Efficient Performance:** Designed for near-zero latency impact, allowing your applications to run smoothly without compromise.
- **Secure and Reliable**: DokuMetry SDKs prioritize the safety and integrity of your data. All data transmission to Doku is conducted over secure HTTP, ensuring that your observability data is protected during transit.
